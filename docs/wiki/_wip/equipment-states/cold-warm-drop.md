# Cold/Warm Drop

## Description
---

When Link drops an object from his inventory, whether it's an item or a piece of equipment, it can either be a cold drop or a warm drop.
A previously equipped piece of equipment will be warm when dropped, and any other object (items and equipment from the inventory) will be cold when dropped.

The main difference between warm and cold drops 

### Changing drop temperature

It is not possible to turn a cold drop into a warm drop.
However the other way around is possible, simply have an enemy pick up the warm drop and have it drop it again. The enemy will drop it cold.
