# Drop Smuggle

A [smuggle](uid:TGY) in the drop [equip position](uid:S8I).

Note that [drop _equips_](uid:HY9) are sometimes referred to as Drop Smuggles, despite not being true smuggles.

## Effects

Drop Smuggles, unlike their static/dynamic counterparts, can be equipped.

- Equipping a Drop Smuggle does nothing to affect its smuggle status; it's just also equipped.
- If you swap from a Drop Smuggle to something else, and you have no \[unculled\] drop zuggles, it will be unequipped without being deleted, preserving its smuggle status.
	- Unequipping will just delete the Drop Smuggle.

## Methods

[Search for drop smuggle methods](search:Drop Smuggle)

## Converting to a zuggle

Equip and warm-drop any other equipment of the same type. The drop smuggle will become a drop zuggle.

## Undoing

To undo a drop smuggle, equip it and fail drop it.

Unlike with other equip positions, dropping, throwing, or shock-dropping active equipment doesn't affect drop smuggles.
