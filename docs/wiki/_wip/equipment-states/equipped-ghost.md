# Equipped Ghost

An equipped item that has lost all physics behaviours, aside from those that come from Link, due to being snapped off from a fuse parent.

## Method

1. Overload drop the target item.
1. Fuse the overload dropped item to a weapon (for shields and bows) or shield (for weapons and bows).
1. Fail drop the item to make it an equipped ghost.

## Effects

- Equipped ghosts largely behave like normal equipment for as long as they remain equipped.
- Their size is influenced by the previous fuse parent, so they usually appear larger than normal.
- Dropping the equipment will ghost it; it will float in mid-air and never do anything ever again. You can't interact with it in basically any way.
- You can freely drop and re-equip the original fuse parent.
