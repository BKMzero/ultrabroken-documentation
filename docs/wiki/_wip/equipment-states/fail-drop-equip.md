# Fail Drop Equip

Fail dropping equipment in a specific way can cause it to reattach to Link, but with the pickup prompt directly accessible. A Fail Drop Zuggle is just a zuggled version of this.

Not to be confused with a [Drop Equip](../drop-equip); the 'drop' here is part of 'fail drop'.

## Effects

Earlier glitch resources may call this a fail drop _smuggle_, although this isn't an actual smuggle in [the sense we understand it now](../smuggle).
