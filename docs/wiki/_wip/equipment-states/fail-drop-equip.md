# Fail Drop Equip

Fail dropping equipment in a specific way can cause it to reattach to Link, but with the pickup prompt directly accessible.

Any action that repositions the equipment on Link will cancel this state, reverting it to normal.

## Terminology

- Earlier glitch resources may call this a fail drop _smuggle_, although this isn't an actual smuggle in [the sense we understand it now](uid:TGY).
- A [Fail Drop Zuggle](uid:PM4) is just a zuggled version of this.

## Methods

[Fail Drop Equip methods](uid:D2J)

## Effects

- The pickup prompt for the equipment is acessible from most angles, and will respond to a lot of in-game A presses.
- Warping with a fail drop equip will remove the pickup prompt and re-equip the item _statically_ to Link's back.
