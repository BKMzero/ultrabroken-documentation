# Invismuggle

An invismuggle is a culled smuggle. See [the page on smuggle](uid:TGY) for more.

## Effects

- As an active smuggle, it carries risk of D-Pad Lock.
- Unculling the invismuggle reverts it to a standard smuggle.
- Unlike with their unculled counterparts, new fusions to weapons and shields don't fuse entangle to invismuggles.

## Upgrading to a zuggle

To upgrade an invismuggle to an invizuggle, you just need to **fail drop** some other equipment. If you're not contending with D-Pad Lock, this will be as easy as it seems. If you are stuck in D-Pad Lock, the only way to do this (without losing the smuggle) is to do the following:

- Have something else of that type equipped (you need to set this up before invismuggling)
- Fill up the invismuggle type's equipment tab
- Obtain a chest containing new equipment of that type
- Move the chest so you can open it while Link's back is to a wall
- Open the chest and try to drop your active equipment of that type, which will fail drop

Fully dropping some other equipment will not turn the invismuggle into an invizuggle, but will instead send it to culled purgatory.
