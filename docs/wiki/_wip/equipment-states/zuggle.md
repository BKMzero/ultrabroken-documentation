# Zuggle

Equipment that was supposed to drop, but never completed, nor got re-equipped properly. Zuggled equipment persists across reloads, making it an extremely useful equipment state. See the [zuggle section](uid:ESW) for more.

Most zuggles are in the static [equip position](uid:S8I), as this is the easiest to execute, and very often good enough.

## Undoing

To undo a static zuggle, equip and warm drop some other equipment of the same type. Throwing or shock-dropping active equipment won't affect static zuggles.
