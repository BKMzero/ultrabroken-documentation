# Zuggle

Fail-dropped equipment that gets neither fully re-equipped, nor stowed into the inventory. Zuggles also transfer across reloads. See the [zuggle section](uid:ESW) for more.

## Static Zuggle

Most zuggles are in the static equip position, as this is the easiest to execute, and very often good enough.

## Undoing

To undo a static zuggle, equip and warm drop some other equipment of the same type. Throwing or shock-dropping active equipment won't affect static zuggles.
