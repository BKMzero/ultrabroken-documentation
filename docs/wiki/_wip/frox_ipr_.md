_mulberry - 22 January 2026_

## Instructions
---
### Base Setup
1. Setup zuggle overload
2. Make an EDI shield
3. Use overload cold fuse to cold fuse a rocket to weapon A
4. Fuse entangle the rocket to the DI shield
5. Full fuse the rocket to weapon A and quickly drop it and equip another weapon
6. The shield will now be an IPR shield as long as you bring A with you.

### Extensions
Frox enemy IPR, a frox IPR setup with an enemy that gives a pocket rocket that has no downsides (infinite use, durability, surfing, range, no battery usage) besides not surviving through loading screens if it is not zuggled
1. Preform the first two steps of frox IPR
2. Smuggle the frox shield
3. Overload drop a shield and fuse it to a weapon
4. Fuse a rocket to that shield
5. Fail drop the oveload dropped shield and drop the weapon it is fused to
6. Equip a new weapon and fuse the rocket to it. This new weapon cannot be deleted or the IPR will be lost, so you may pick an already pslotted weapon.
7. Overload pick up this new weapon and drop your equipped shield
8. Give the rocket weapon to an enemy
9. With link's back to a wall, pick up the weapon with a fused shield, quickly pause, and then drop you equipped weapon, equip a new weapon, and unequip it
10. Take the rocket weapon back with a dazzlefruit or by killing the enemy
11. Send the weapon to culled purgatory or recall lock it to give the IPR infinite range
