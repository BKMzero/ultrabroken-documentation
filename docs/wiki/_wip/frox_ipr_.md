## Instructions
---
### Base Setup
1. Setup zuggle overload
2. Make an EDI shield
3. Use overload cold fuse to cold fuse a rocket to weapon A
4. Fuse entangle the rocket to the DI shield
5. Full fuse the rocket to weapon A and quickly drop it and equip another weapon
6. The shield will now be an IPR shield as long as you bring A with you.
