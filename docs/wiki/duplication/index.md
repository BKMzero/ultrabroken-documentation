## Introduction
Duplication refers to glitches that allow players to create multiple copies of items, weapons, objects, or resources in-game. These glitches exploit the game's item management and spawning systems to bypass normal inventory and equipment constraints. Duplication techniques range from simple hold-over methods to complex state manipulation involving culling, inventory swaps, and entity destruction. Mastering duplication is essential for sequence breaking, enabling infinite resources, and unlocking advanced glitching routes in Tears of the Kingdom.
