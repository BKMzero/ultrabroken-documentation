# Hold Storage Duplication

## Summary
---
Allows you to dupe items via hold storage.

_NaN Gogh, Mozz - 3 July 2023_

## Instructions
---
1. Activate hold storage onto a horse.
2. From on top of the horse, throw all of the item onto the ground until you have none left.
3. Jump off the horse and drop the hold stored items onto the ground.
4. Pick up all your items.

## Notes
---
Also known as Minus Dupe.
