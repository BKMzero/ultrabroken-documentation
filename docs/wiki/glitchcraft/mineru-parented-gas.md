# Mineru Parented GAS

## Summary
A variation of cull GAS that can be performed entirely using Mineru-related tech.

_WinnerBoi77 - 15 March 2024_

## Instructions
1. Setup portable culling
2. Fuse entangle a battery-consuming zonai device to Mineru
3. Fuse the same device to a shield/weapon
4. Prompt Mineru to cull, then quickly swing/guard with the fused equipment before she unculls
5. Fuse something to Mineru on the same slot that the zonai device is occupied by to detangle it
6. (optional) Zuggle the device-fused weapon to make GAS go through saves
