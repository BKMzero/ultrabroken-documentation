# MNF Fusing

## Summary
Allows you to fuse to the MNF

_LegendofLinkk - 5 June 2023_

## Instructions

### "Method 1 (`1.0.0`–`1.1.2`)"
	1. Equip your unfused MNF and drop the desired fuse in front of Link
	2. Activate Fuse and open the inventory
	3. Drop the MNF and equip any other unfused weapon
	4. Unpause and immediately press Y (this can be done through spamming the Y button while in the inventory)
	5. If the equipped weapon has an invisble fuse, and the MNF is now named "" instead of "MsgNotFound" on the ground, pick up the MNF, if not, retry

### "Method 2 (Fuse Storage, all versions)"
	This method only works with fused items that cull in culling areas.

	1. Equip a random unfused weapon
	1. Perform Fuse Storage on your intended fuse target
	1. Swap to the MNF
	1. Enter the culling area to trigger the stored fuse

### "Method 3 (Transitive Zuggle FE, all versions)"
	!!!danger Replacement Actors
		This method doesn't work with any item that replaces its actor when fused (any item that has a single use when fused is a replacement actor, but there are others). Attempting it anyway will crash the game!

	1. Zuggle the MNF
	1. Equip any other unfused weapon
	1. Fuse the intended item to your equipped weapon
	1. Drop your equipped weapon to release the MNF zuggle
	1. Pick up the properly fused weapon, then the MNF

	The fused item will be deleted from your equipped weapon, but will still be registered on the MNF in your inventory.

### "Method 4 (Overload FE, all versions)"

	!!!danger Replacement Actors
		This method doesn't work with any item that replaces its actor when fused (any item that has a single use when fused is a replacement actor, but there are others). Attempting it anyway will crash the game!

	This method will consume a weapon (although zuggle overload makes it easy to dupe it first).

	1. Have an unfused shield equipped
	1. Zuggle the MNF
	1. Equip another unfused weapon while overloaded, so that it drops
	1. Fuse the overload dropped weapon to your shield
	1. Fuse the target item to your weapon
	1. Fail drop your equipped weapon, then drop it properly
		- The weapon will ghost, and you won't be able to pick it up
	1. Pick up and unequip the MNF

## Notes
- You can also fuse to the MNF using any unload method of Weapon State Transfer.
